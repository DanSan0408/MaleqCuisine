Our Story

Founded in the heart of **Bahau, Negeri Sembilan**, Maleq Cuisine began with a simple mission: to serve honest, flavorful hot food that brings people together.

What started as a small family stall has grown into a beloved local institution. We specialize in signature grilled meats, authentic Malaysian hot dishes, and our famous session-based delivery system that ensures your meal arrives at the peak of its temperature and taste.
